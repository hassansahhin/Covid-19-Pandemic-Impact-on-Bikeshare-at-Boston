Coronavirus has had a large-scale impact on transportation. This report attempts to assess the effects of COVID-19 on biking.

We will focus on the impact of the Pandemic on two main variables:

The Original biking data extracted from https://www.bluebikes.com/system-data

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset showing the information of more than one million bike share trips in Boston in two period of times

consist of 14 column covering ( trip duration , trip distination , number of trips and user information) distibuted in 1,026,065 rows tripduration - quantitative continuous variable the remaining columns are categorical nominal variable ('start station name','end station name', 'bikeid', 'usertype', 'birth year', 'gender','month', 'day', 'hour', 'year', 'Trip').
